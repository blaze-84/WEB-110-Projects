Important Facts

  1. Queer people experience high rates of depression and anxiety.
  2. 1 in 8 Queer people ages 18-24 have attempted suicide.
  3. Almost half of Trans people have considered suicide.
  4. "Findings show 7% of LGB adults age 18-59 in the U.S. have experienced conversion therapy."
  5. Half of the LGBTQ+ community in the US experiences estrangement from their family

Understanding Mental Health

Mental health is an essential part of overall wellbeing. The LGBTQIA+ community faces unique challenges that impact mental health.

Support Systems

Having a strong support system is crucial for mental health. This includes friends, family, therapists, and community groups.

Seeking Help

If you or someone you know is struggling, reaching out for help is a sign of strength. There are many resources available specifically for the LGBTQIA+ community.

Below are some helpful resources for LGBTQ+ and their loved ones.

  • Trevor Project: 1-866-488-7386
  • Gay & Lesbian Alliance Against Defamation (GLAAD)
  • Trans LifeLine 1-877-565-8860
  • Human Rights Campaign 1 (800) 727-4723
  • LGBT National Hotline: 1-888-843-4564
  • Transmission Ministry Collective
